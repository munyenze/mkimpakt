Who We Are

We Know Kenya.
So We Do Kenya.

MK Impakt Advisory Limited is a Kenyan-owned specialist advisory firm dedicated to supporting government institutions, international development agencies, and the private sector to understand, measure, and maximise their social and economic impact.

"MK Impakt Advisory was founded on the recognised need within the international development community to ground and prioritize project interventions in local context — ensuring programmes are designed, implemented, and evaluated with a deep understanding of the communities they serve."

Our team combines national expertise with international development agency experience, working alongside a network of national and international experts. We are registered as a Private Limited Company in Kenya and are fully compliant with EU GDPR and Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019.

Our Mission

To help organisations understand, measure, and maximise their social and economic impact through rigorous evidence, contextualised strategy, and digital and AI-powered innovation.

Our Vision

A development landscape where every intervention is grounded in real-world data, where communities' outcomes are prioritised, and where Africa's knowledge ecosystem grows stronger with every programme cycle.

Our Approach

We contextualise global frameworks to local realities — building SMART indicators, supporting donor reporting, and enabling adaptive management that serves both partners and communities across Africa.

How We Work

Our Approach &
Methodology

At MK Impakt Advisory, we believe that effective development programming begins with a rigorous understanding of context. Our methodology integrates three interdependent pillars.

01

Evidence-Based Design

We co-design MEL frameworks, logframes, and results-based management systems grounded in internationally recognised standards (OECD DAC, EU, World Bank) and adapted to the Kenyan and regional context.

02

Digital-First Implementation

We integrate digital MEL tools enabling real-time data capture, automated aggregation, and customisable reporting — reducing reporting burden while improving data quality and accountability.

03

Adaptive Learning

We embed learning loops into every programme cycle — from baseline to evaluation — ensuring evidence informs decision-making, programme adaptation, and knowledge sharing with partners and donors.

What We Do

Our Core Services

From strategy design to on-the-ground implementation, our services span the full programme management cycle.

01

Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning

Design and implementation of M&E strategies, frameworks, and logframes aligned with global targets. We support the full MEL cycle: data collection, analysis, periodic reporting, and lessons-learned dissemination.

02

Project Advisory & Management

Technical and strategic advisory on programme design, contextualisation, and implementation — from inception through to close-out, with a focus on sustainability and local ownership.

03

Strategy & Impact Measurement

Development of impact measurement systems, theories of change, and results frameworks that connect programme activities to intended outcomes within the Kenyan and African development environment.

04

Research & Analysis

Integrated qualitative and quantitative research spanning baseline, mid-term, and endline evaluations. We design tools, manage field teams, and deliver analysis to inform programme adaptation and donor reporting.

05

Stakeholder Engagement & Facilitation

Planning and facilitation of delegation missions, policy dialogues, multi-donor coordination workshops, and work planning sessions that translate into actionable outputs.

06

Capacity Development

Hands-on training and institutional capacity building for M&E teams, government counterparts, and implementing partners — building sustainable local expertise in MEL systems and digital tools.

eLearning
Training & Capacity Development Platform

Data Skills Training & Customisable Partner Courses

MK Impakt Advisory offers access to a dedicated e-learning platform designed to strengthen data literacy and digital skills across development organisations and their programme beneficiaries. The platform delivers structured, self-paced training on data collection, management, analysis, and reporting — equipping teams with the practical skills needed to maximise the value of their MEL systems.

A key feature of the platform is its customisation capability: implementing partners and organisations can upload their own bespoke courses, allowing them to deliver tailored training content directly to their target groups — whether field staff, community enumerators, government counterparts, or programme beneficiaries — all within a single managed environment.
